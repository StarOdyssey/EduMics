Modern Educational Microscope M620ED

 

Digital microscope 

 

 

 

 

MediWorks’s  microscope M620ED allows you to see the details and structure of the observed object to improve the quality of researches. The zoom and multifunction knobs of the microscope can enhance the visualization of details of an object. The educational microscope is an indispensable instrument for researcher. MediWorks can provide researchers and students with affordable solutions.

 

 

 

Advantages of Microscope

 

Single finger control

 

 

 

 

 

Without leaving the handle, you can reach out the knob and objective lens of the educational microscope with one single finger to achieve minimal control.

 

 

Multi-functional knob

The light source modes in the M620ED multi-function knob adjusts the power of white light source.

White light source

Using the best LED light source with high CRI, excellent color restoration, and uniformity of lighting spot.

Objective

The working distance of educational microscope M620ED if fixed to 250 mm

Continuous zoom system

MediWorks’s microscope can adjust the magnification continuously without interruption, effectively reducing the discomfort caused by the switching of the field of view, allowing the researcher to focus more on the treatment.
Continuous magnification system of 0.7 x to 2.0 x. Continuously view at the highest magnification without over or under-sizing the viewing field.

Information Display

M620ED can visually display the light source mode to feedback the zoom factor in real time, display the brightness adjustment status, and create a better environment.

Cutting-edge Optical Lens

Advanced optical lens with Apochromatic Design (APO), able to remove color difference, improve articulation index and increase the depth of field.

Ergonomic Design

The tiltable handle of the microscope can be ergonomically designed to bring a more comfortable operation to the researcher.

5 in 1 Digital Module

Integrate 30°extender, beam splitter, rotation ring, HD camera and iris diaphragm, not only improves the optical performance, but also reduces the weight of external accessories. Internal HD camera with lower noise. The iris diaphragm is able to control the depth of field.

 

 

 

 

 

Educational Microscope Parts

Except for the 5 in 1 module of M620ED, any kinds of accessories are offered, either, and an upgrade of educational microscope would be achieved. Various objectives, Binocular extender, digital camera adapter, 5 in 1 module, Binocular extender & beam splinter, CCD adapter, Rotation Ring, Digital camera interface, 3D mobile phone interface are optional.
